About Convertly

Your go-to platform for quick and easy file conversions

Our Mission

Convertly aims to provide a simple, fast, and secure way to convert files between various formats without any hassle. We believe that file conversion should be accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

How It Works

Using Convertly is incredibly easy:

  1. Select the conversion type you need from our homepage
  2. Upload your file (we support files up to 100MB)
  3. Wait a few seconds while we process your conversion
  4. Download your converted file

All conversions happen in your browser, ensuring your files never leave your device and providing maximum privacy and security.

Supported Formats

Convertly supports a wide range of file formats, including:

  • Documents: DOC, DOCX, PDF, ODT, TXT

  • Spreadsheets: XLS, XLSX, CSV,

  • Images: JPG, PNG,

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. That's why:

  • All file conversions happen directly in your browser
  • Your files are never stored on our servers
  • We don't collect any personal data from your files
  • Files are automatically deleted after conversion

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Convertly completely free?

Yes, Convertly is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees or premium features that require payment.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, currently we support files up to 100MB. This limit allows us to provide fast conversions while maintaining quality service.

How do you ensure my files' privacy?

All conversions happen directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Currently, we support single file conversion. Batch conversion is on our roadmap for future updates.

What happens if my conversion fails?

If your conversion fails, we recommend trying again with a different file. If issues persist, please contact us for assistance.

How can I request a new conversion format?

We're always looking to expand our supported formats. Please use the contact form to suggest new conversion types.
